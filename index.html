Pipeline at a glance

Camera captures reality, the model detects objects, data is sent to the phone for context, and the OLED overlays the insight. Bluetooth keeps the glasses and phone in sync.

01

Camera intake

Frame stream enters the embedded vision block for feature extraction.

02

Object detection

Detectors classify objects, names are passed to the HUD buffer.

03

Phone relay

Bidirectional BLE channel moves names and phone notifications.

04

OLED output

128 x 64 OLED prints the object label and mirrored notifications.
